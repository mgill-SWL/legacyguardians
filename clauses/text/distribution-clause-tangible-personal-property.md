# Distribution Clause - Tangible Personal Property

The Trustee shall distribute, free of trust, such items of my tangible personal property as may then be included in the Trust Estate in accordance with any written instructions left by me and the remainder of such personal property, or all of it if no such instructions are left, to my children in equal shares, in the trustee’s discretion.

All costs of safeguarding, insuring, storing, and delivering the tangible personal property to the beneficiaries entitled thereto shall be paid out of the Trust Estate as an expense of administration
