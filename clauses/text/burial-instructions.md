# Burial Instructions

Burial Instructions

Pure Agent’s Discretion
I wish to be scattered or interred in a way that is meaningful to my family in my agent’s discretion.

Catholic Burial

I direct that my funeral and burial be carried out according to the rites of the Roman Catholic Church, including a Vigil/Prayer Service, Funeral Mass, and Rite of Committal as practicable. I direct that I be buried (not cremated, unless consistent with Catholic teaching and my family’s judgment) in a Catholic cemetery or consecrated ground, with appropriate prayers and blessings. I ask that my family consult my parish priest for the liturgy, readings, and music, and that my burial be reverent, simple, and faithful to Catholic tradition.

Islamic Burial
I direct that my body be prepared, washed, and shrouded in accordance with Islamic rites (ghusl and kafan) and handled with dignity and modesty by those qualified to do so. I direct that my funeral prayer (Salat al-Janazah) be offered promptly and that I be buried as soon as practicable in a Muslim cemetery or section, in conformity with Islamic practice.  I direct that my grave be oriented toward the Qibla (facing Makkah) and that my burial be simple, without embalming or cremation unless required by law.

Military burial (general)
I direct that I be accorded military funeral honors consistent with my service, including flag presentation, honor guard, and, if available, “Taps.” I direct that my remains be interred in a veterans cemetery or other cemetery permitting military honors, with my military service noted on the grave marker. I request that my family work with the appropriate military funeral honors office and the Department of Veterans Affairs to arrange all eligible rites and benefits.

Variation — interment at Arlington National Cemetery (by right of rank/eligibility)
Pursuant to my eligibility by rank and service, I direct that application be made for my interment at Arlington National Cemetery with full military honors as authorized. If interment at Arlington is approved, I direct that my remains be buried there in accordance with Arlington and military protocols, including flag presentation and honors. If Arlington interment is not approved or is impracticable, I direct interment in a veteran’s cemetery of my family’s choosing with the same military honors.

Family Plot
I direct that my remains be interred in the [Family Name] family burial plot located at [Cemetery Name], Section [] / Lot [], alongside my relatives in accordance with our family’s tradition. I request that my family and personal representative coordinate with the cemetery to confirm the specific space within the family plot and to arrange all opening/closing and marker details. If the family plot is unavailable despite reasonable efforts, I direct interment in a nearby plot within the same cemetery, or if none, in a comparable location selected by my family.

Specific Plot
I direct that my remains be interred in my designated burial plot at [Cemetery Name], Section [] / Lot [] / Space [__], or such successor designation as the cemetery records reflect. If that plot is unavailable despite reasonable efforts, I direct interment in the nearest available plot within the same cemetery, or if none, in a comparable plot selected by my family.
