# Charitable Distribution Cy Pres

Charitable Distribution Cy Pres

If any charitable beneficiary named herein is not in existence, has ceased to qualify as an organization described in Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, or its purposes have become impossible, impracticable, or illegal to carry out, the Trustee shall distribute such property to one or more charitable organizations that, in the Trustee’s judgment, most closely fulfill the charitable purposes intended by the Grantor, applying principles analogous to cy pres without necessity of court approval.
