# Pour-Over Holographic Will Template

Holographic Pour-Over Will Instructions
Write your Will entirely in your own handwriting. Headers, footers, everything on the next page. It does not have to be in cursive script unless you wanted it to be. Absolutely no part of the Will may be pre-printed language - do not print out and sign the template. You may of course print out the template to reference as you write your will, just don’t sign the template.
As you write the will, supply the information indicated by the bolded brackets. The name of your Trust is located on page one of your living trust, e.g.: “The Michael Gill Living Trust”. Include the date the trust was electronically notarized in the following format: “dated March 26, 2020”. The full name of the trust you write would therefore be “The Michael Gill Living Trust dated March 26, 2020”
Sign with your normal formal signature, print your name below your signature, and date the document.
Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any questions.

I give my estate to the then-acting Trustee or Trustees of the above-mentioned Trust Agreement, together with any additions or amendments thereto, to be added to the principal of that Trust and to be held, administered and distributed under the Trust Agreement and any amendments to such Trust Agreement.
