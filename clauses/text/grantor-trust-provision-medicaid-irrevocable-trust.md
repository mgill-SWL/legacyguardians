# Grantor Trust Provision - Medicaid Irrevocable Trust

2.K. Grantor Trust Status. It is the intent of the Grantor that this Trust be construed as a “Grantor Trust” under Internal Revenue Code §674(a) (Power to Control Enjoyment) and §677(a) (Income for Benefit of Grantor). All income distributed, held or accumulated by this Trust shall be taxable to the Grantor.
