# Specific Distribution of Pets

The Trustee shall distribute any pets in my possession plus the sum of FIVE THOUSAND DOLLARS ($5,000) per pet to ______________. If all of the aforementioned disclaim this bequest or predeceases me, then I give to my Trustee a power of appointment to select an appropriate beneficiary of this bequest. If I do not own any pets at the time of my passing, then this bequest shall be null and void in its entirety.

I give to my Trustee a power of appointment to select an appropriate beneficiary of any pets in my possession at the time of my passing plus the sum of FIVE THOUSAND DOLLARS ($5,000) per pet. If I do not own any pets at the time of my passing, then this bequest shall be null and void in its entirety.

Pets. A pet shall consist of one animal, except in the case of a community of animals where the individuals may be difficult to discern from one another and which are together treated as a unit for purposes of cleaning, feeding, and maintaining the community, such as an aquarium, or an ant colony. Such a community shall be treated as the equivalent of one pet, so long as doing so does not place an unreasonable financial burden on the beneficiary of a pet bequest.
