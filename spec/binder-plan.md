# Binder Plan (MVP)

This document is the canonical packet order for a **Joint Trust** matter.

## Ordering principle

Signing ceremony order:
1) Trust first (so it exists for transfers)
2) Documents that require witnessing
3) Documents that require notarization
4) Documents that typically do not require signature at the ceremony (client coaching / leave blank)
5) Helpful binder inserts

## Packet order (Joint Trust MVP)

1) **Joint Trust**
   - Includes Exhibit “A” Standard Trustee Powers (not a separate document)

2) **Last Will and Testament**

3) **Advance Medical Directive**

4) **Burial Power of Attorney**

5) **General Durable Power of Attorney**
   - Includes Agent Acknowledgement + Agent’s Certification (not separate documents)

6) **Certification of Trust**
   - With Exhibit “A” Standard Trustee Powers appended

7) **Assignment of Tangible Personal Property**

8) **Declaration of Trust**

9) **Instructions for distribution of tangible personal property**
   - Practice note: usually left blank at signing; provide coaching on use

10) **Summary of Client Information (SCI)**
    - Workflow note: typically the first client deliverable for review/comments

11) **Summary of Estate Planning Provisions**

## Notes

- “Exhibit A / Standard Trustee Powers” should be treated as an *appendix module* that can be included under multiple documents (at least: Joint Trust and Certification of Trust).
- SCI + Summary of EP Provisions are binder inserts and may be generated earlier/later than ceremony.
