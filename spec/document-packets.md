# Legacy Guardians — Document packets

Version: v0.2
Date: 2026-03-05

This document defines **which documents are generated** for each matter type/jurisdiction and what is in scope by stage.

## Stage 1 (Virginia) — Estate Planning baseline packet (in scope)
Jurisdiction: **Virginia (VA)**

### Packet: VA Revocable Living Trust Plan (baseline)
**Goal output quality:** a **spotless** Word-ready packet comparable to the firm’s current deliverable (typically **120–130 pages**).

Documents included (generated by LG):
1. **Summary of Client Information**
   - Enumerates: contact details, family circumstances, fiduciary role selections, and distribution scheme
   - Must include: **Specific distributions** section + **Residual distribution** section
2. **Living Trust**
   - Variants: **joint** trust; **individual reciprocal** trusts
3. **Certification of Trust**
4. **Declaration of Trust**
5. **Assignment of Tangible Personal Property**
6. **Instructions for Distribution of Tangible Personal Property**
7. **Last Will and Testament**
   - Variants: **pour-over** (to trust) **or standalone** will (as configured)
8. **Power of Attorney**
9. **Advance Medical Directive**
10. **HIPAA Authorization**
11. **Final Disposition Authorization + Instructions**
12. **Deed into Trust**

### Packet: VA Incapacity Documents Only
Matter type: **Incapacity Documents Only**

Documents included (generated by LG):
1. **Power of Attorney**
2. **Advance Medical Directive**
3. **HIPAA Authorization**
4. **Final Disposition Authorization + Instructions** *(default included)*

Notes:
- This packet should support assembling a limited-scope representation without requiring the full trust/will questionnaire.
- UX/data capture should use a **slimmed-down questionnaire schema** tailored to incapacity docs.
- LG should also allow **single-document assembly** (e.g., "generate only the POA") as a deterministic subset export of a packet.

### Template and assembly requirements (Stage 1)
- Templates must be **versioned** and linked to jurisdiction (VA) and packet.
- Assembly must support:
  - merge fields,
  - conditional clauses,
  - repeating sections (beneficiaries, fiduciaries, children, assets, real property entries, etc.),
  - deterministic re-runs (same inputs + template version → same output).

### Out of scope / de-prioritized in Stage 1
- **Irrevocable trusts**: explicitly lower priority; treat as **Stage 2+** unless mandated.
- Additional states or county-specific variations beyond the VA templates provided.
