Parking Instructions
Address: 8280 Willow Oaks Corporate Dr., Suite 600, Fairfax, VA 22031
Brief Overview: Our office is on the sixth floor of the building. Garage parking is free. Park in the garage, navigate through the pre-lobby, find the elevators, ride up to the sixth floor, and find suite 600.
Upon entering the garage dedicated to 8280 Willow Oaks Corporate Drive, it is recommended that you park close to the “B” Level of the garage (color-coded red).  Entering the building from this door, you will pass through a small pre-lobby and go through another set of doors, passing a coffee station on your left. You will then encounter the lobby-level elevator bank.  Ride the elevator to the sixth floor, find Suite 600 (immediately in view upon exit from the elevator), and find reception.  Please notify them that you are there for Speedwell Law.

Garage Parking: Willow Oaks Corporate Center is a complex of three buildings off Willow Oaks Corporate Drive. Our building, #8280, will be on the right side as you drive in.  The garage dedicated to the building is attached to and behind (east of) the office building. The garage is labeled “8280”.  Parking is free for visitors, and it is best to park on the “B” level (color-coded red).  
Once parked, go through the door in the garage and head towards the lobby-level elevator bank. There is a pre-lobby with double glass doors to pass through.  You will see the complimentary coffee bar/refreshment area to your left if you’re running early!  		
Ride the elevator to the sixth floor.  Upon exit from the elevator, you will see Suite 600, a Regus office. The front desk receptionist is Grace. Ask for Speedwell Law.
