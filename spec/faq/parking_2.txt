Street Parking: Park on Dulaney St. or Jamieson Ave. Parking is $1.75/hr, up to 2 hour parking using the ParkMobile App.
	Once parked, walk up Dulany St. towards Duke St. The building entrance is on Dulany almost at the corner of Duke St. (black arrow). Enter the building then:
-	Locate the elevator bank to the right beyond the security desk.
-	The security guard is Rodney. He probably won’t challenge you. If you need to explain for any reason, just let him know you’re headed to the third floor for Speedwell Law.
-	Use the elevator and go to the third floor. The reception desk is to the right. Ask for Speedwell Law at the reception desk. If a receptionist is not available, you’re welcome to take a seat or use the restroom (to the right of reception, around the corner).
